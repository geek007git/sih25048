LearnQuiz is a Tech Quiz App designed especially for students to make learning fun and interactive.

Students can learn through educational videos, engage with games that reinforce concepts, and attempt MCQs at the end of each learning session to test their knowledge.

The app also features a leaderboard to encourage healthy competition, along with progress tracking so students can visualize their improvement over time.

This is Phase I of the project, where we lay the foundation for an engaging and effective learning experience.
